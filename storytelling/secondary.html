🤖 🤖 Storytime with Alpha-Mini 🤖 🤖 !


Welcome to Alpha-Mini Story Generation. We believe that storytelling has a huge power in teaching and children
developpement . Hence this model promotes two main functions: generating stories for different age group and levels,
and storyfying the teaching course content with the help of artificial intelligence while storyfying the content.
While this tailor-made models does storytelling with the robot Alpha-Mini, it is also possible to ask questions to children
about the stories, and to create complete dialogs with the students. On the other side, the control is still completely on the
user! At any point in time, user can modify or give feedback to the model to change the path of this generative model!
Let's explore this journey together!
